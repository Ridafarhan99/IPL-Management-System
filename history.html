HISTORICAL MOMENTS

BACKGROUND

1

The Indian Cricket League (ICL) was founded in 2007, with funding provided by Zee Entertainment Enterprises.

2

The ICL was not recognised by the Board of Control for Cricket in India (BCCI) or the International Cricket Council (ICC).

3

BCCI increased the prize money in their own domestic tournaments and imposed lifetime bans on players joining the ICL.

FOUNDATION

1

On 13 September 2007, the BCCI announced the launch of a franchise-based Twenty20 cricket competition called Indian Premier League.

2

BCCI vice-president Lalit Modi, said to be the mastermind behind the idea of the IPL, along with the details of the format and rules.

3

The league's format was similar to that of the Premier League of England and the NBA in the United States.

EXPANSION

1

On 21 March 2010, it was announced that two new franchises – Pune Warriors India and Kochi Tuskers Kerala – would join fourth season in 2011.

2

Sahara Adventure Sports Group bought the Pune franchise for $370 million while Rendezvous Sports World bought the Kochi franchise for $333.3 million.

3

On 8 December 2015, it was revealed that Rising Pune Supergiant and the Gujarat Lions would replace Chennai and Rajasthan for two seasons.

TERMINATION

1

On 2011 end, it was announced that the Kochi Tuskers Kerala side would be terminated following the side breaching the BCCI's terms of conditions.

2

Pune Warriors India withdrew from the IPL on May 2013 over financial crisis later the franchise was officially terminated by the BCCI, on October 2013.

3

On June 2015, news came Chennai Super Kings and Rajasthan Royals would be suspended for two seasons for match-fixing and betting scandal.

CONTROVERSY

1

Off-spinner Harbhajan Singh, of Mumbai Indians, was trapped in a major controversy after he was pictured slapping pacer S.Sreesanth in Mohali.

2

After the 2008 Mumbai attacks the Indian board decided to keep the Pakistani cricketers away from the Indian Premier League.

3

IPL Chairman Lalit Modi sacked as it was a huge blow and the biggest controversy till then as he was charged on five counts.
